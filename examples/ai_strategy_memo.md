# Internal memo: automation roadmap

We should prioritise automation in places where failure is visible and reversible. Customer support triage, document routing, and internal knowledge search are good first candidates. Claims about productivity gains should be measured locally rather than borrowed from vendor slides.

The main risk is not that the model gives a wrong answer once. The bigger risk is that the organisation forgets where judgement has moved. Every automation project should name its owner, failure mode, rollback plan, and monitoring metric.
