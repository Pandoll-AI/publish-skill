# AI agents and work

In today's fast-paced world, AI agents are a game-changing paradigm shift for every organization. They will optimize all knowledge work by 2026 and make traditional software teams obsolete because agents can analyze tasks and complete them without supervision.

My view is more practical. The question is not whether agents matter, but where they should be placed in a workflow. Teams need to decide which decisions are automated, which decisions are only assisted, and which decisions remain human.

This article explores why leaders must adopt the latest models immediately. The best companies will be the ones that move fastest.
